## 7. DISCOUNT STRATEGIES

### Volume Licensing Tiers
- **Enterprise Agreement (EA)**: Minimum 500 users, typically 15-20% discount
- **Enterprise Agreement Subscription (EAS)**: 3-year commitment, additional 5-10% discount
- **Cloud Solution Provider (CSP)**: Flexible monthly billing, 5-15% partner discounts

### Azure Savings Programs
- **Reserved Instances (1-year)**: 40% savings
- **Reserved Instances (3-year)**: 72% savings
- **Azure Savings Plans**: Commit to hourly spend, flexible across services
- **Azure Hybrid Benefit**: Bring existing Windows Server/SQL licenses

### Seasonal Promotions
- **End of Microsoft Fiscal Year (June)**: Best time for negotiations
- **Q4 Calendar Year**: Additional discounts often available
- **New customer offers**: Sometimes waived onboarding/migration costs
